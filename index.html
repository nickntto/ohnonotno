ABOUT US

No Shop is a collaboration between Amber Nicole Gavin, Leah Edwina Martin and the community. Opening in the fall of 2009 as an experiment on the sustainability of curating unique vintage pieces and classic basics. NO SHOP caters to anyone who enjoys vintage shopping, the unique subcultures of San Francisco and aims to elevate vintage clothing as a great alternative to buying something new. The shop is located in the Mission District, off 16th and Valencia, surrounded by places to shop, eat and drink, making it a destination for locals and tourists alike. Don’t be surprised if you’ll walk out with a bag full of new threads and timeless pieces.
